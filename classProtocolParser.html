Detailed Description

Stateless utility for serializing and deserializing Packets.

Wraps the Packet wire format (pipe-delimited) behind a clean API. Used by UT-BE-03 (deserialize all 6 types) and UT-BE-04 (round-trip). Throws ProtocolException on malformed input.
